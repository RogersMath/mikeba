Project Senegal

This page is dedicated to a simple but powerful idea: that every child in Senegal, and across the globe, deserves access to a world-class education. This mission is inspired by the tireless work of educators like Mike Ba, who are leveraging the power of the internet to open up new worlds of learning for their students. This is a starting point, a curated list of free online resources to help make this vision a reality.

Essential Learning Tools

The internet is a vast library of knowledge. Here are some of the best free resources for learning, from foundational skills to creative exploration.

  • Khan Academy

    A non-profit educational organization that provides free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere. It offers practice exercises, instructional videos, and a personalized learning dashboard.

  • OER Commons

    A public digital library of open educational resources (OER). OER Commons allows users to explore, create, and collaborate with educators around the world to improve curriculum.

  • Scratch

    A visual programming language and online community where children can create their own interactive stories, games, and animations. It's a great introduction to coding.

  • GCompris

    A high-quality educational software suite with a large number of activities for children aged 2 to 10. Some activities are game-oriented, but still educational.

  • Tux Paint

    A free, award-winning drawing program for children ages 3 to 12 with a simple, easy-to-use interface and fun sound effects.

  • eduActiv8

    A free and open-source educational tool for children, offering a variety of interactive learning activities across multiple subjects.
